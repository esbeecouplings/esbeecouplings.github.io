Introduction

Please note that ESBI is no longer known as "ESBI-VULKAN". ESBI no more manufacture VULKAN branded coupling or VULKAN Spares. According to "Global Settlement Agreement" between VULKAN KUPPLUNGS -und GETRIEBEBAU Bernhard Hackforth GmbH & Co. KG / Germany and ESBI and others, we no more produce VULKAN EZ spares or VULKAN EZS spares or VULKAN EZR spare, neither we use the code no. of series EZ, EZS and EZR. We do have alternative code nos. of our products, which carry the brand "ESBI" only.

ESBI Highly Flexible Couplings have been successful in the field of Power transmission since the early 1970's. Primarily, the success story is ascribed to the torque-transmitting element; a pair of identical rubber diaphragms, reinforced with fabric plies and steel laminations to produce flexibility and durability for optimum torque transmission.

ESBI has references, all over the world, that they have been successful in supplying the highly flexible couplings & spare rubber elements in marine main propulsion drives, generator drives and auxiliary drives. Our in-house test facilities shall prove that quality of our products is at par with German manufacturers and we do give warranty for supply of spares also.

Salient Features

  • Multidirectional flexibility

  • High damping characteristics.

  • Low dynamic torsional stiffness resulting in low natural frequency of the drive system thus keeping the critical speeds below the operation speeds

  • Rupture Torque is about 6 to 9 times the nominal torque

  • Needs no lubrication

  • Torque range from 22Kgm to 30,700Kgm.

Applications

ESBI's enormous reference list on Highly Flexible Couplings includes Marine Main Propulsion, Diesel Driven Generators, Pumps and compressors, arduous applications in Cement & Steel Plants, Paper & Sugar Mills, Textiles & Chemical Industries, Oil Refineries, Locomotive Drives, etc.

Varoflex Coupling & Spares

Varoflex Coupling equivalent and interchangeable with RATO-S Coupling, was developed by ESBI with direct support from TATA, a renowned group of industries, in India and around the world. Such RATO-S coupling manufactured by Vulkan Kupplungs, Germany was IMPORTED and fitted with WARTSILA Diesel Engine Model 18V46. We are obliged to TATA for providing the scope and support encouraging ESBI's R&D; to achieve an import substitution. Tata's stringent quality checking parameters and rigorous testing in the practical applications was great encouragement and impetus to ESBI's technical team which achieved the success story.

Since the RATO-S Coupling and spares are not covered under any Indian Patent laws, we are continuously developing and manufacturing the same with our own Research and Development and our registered Brand name is "VAROFLEX". ESBI's Varoflex is developed with 100% indigenous resources, technologies under ESBI'S own "R&D;" and spontaneous customers support.

Varoflex Spare (Rubber Segment)

First prototype replacement of such RATO-S Coupling Spare Size V- 582 were developed and supplied to TATA during the year 2009. Since then, TATA being a satisfied customer, ESBI enjoyed regular support and repeat orders for replacing all such imported RATO-S Couplings with ESBI VAROFLEX spares.

ESBI also successfully replaced RATO-S Coupling spares with indigenously manufactured VAROFLEX spares, installed in Marine Main Propulsion applications.

Hytrex Coupling

Another success story that replaces thousands of KTR Brand FLE-PA Couplings :- ESBI's Hytrex Couplings, 100% indigenously developed, replacing KTR, FLE-PA Couplings, imported in India. Customers like Larsen & Toubro, Telcon, Greaves etc. were of great support to develop such import substitution with their orders replacing KTR FLE-PA Coupling spares, in India.

The Disc being the only replaceable material, ESBI has used Hytrex Polymer with higher physical strength and temperature durability. Flange dimensions are according to SAE-J620 to replace existing installation of KTR, as desired by various customers from time to time.
